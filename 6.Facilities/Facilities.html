Facilities
~ FOOD ~

Nutritious meals are thoughtfully provided for all school children, ensuring they receive the essential nourishment for their overall well-being and academic success. Our commitment to fostering a healthy learning environment includes the provision of balanced and wholesome meals daily.

~ SWIMMING POOL ~

The government school features a swimming pool as part of its facilities, providing students with a recreational and fitness resource to enhance their overall educational experience. This aquatic facility contributes to a well-rounded and active school environment.

~ BADMINTON COURT ~

The school is equipped with a badminton court, offering students a designated space for recreational and sports activities. This facility enhances physical well-being and fosters a sense of teamwork and competition within the school community.

~ AUDITORIUM ~

The school houses an auditorium, serving as a versatile space for various events and gatherings. From academic presentations to cultural performances, this facility provides a conducive environment for students to showcase their talents and for the school community to come together for shared experiences and celebrations.

~ COMPUTER LAB ~

The school features a dedicated computer lab, equipped with modern technology to facilitate hands-on learning and skill development in the field of computer science. This resourceful space allows students to explore digital literacy, coding, and other technological applications, fostering a dynamic and forward-thinking educational environment.

~ PHYSICS LAB ~

The school's physics lab is a hub for hands-on experimentation and scientific exploration. Fitted with cutting-edge equipment and apparatus, the lab provides students with a practical platform to delve into the principles of physics. Here, they engage in experiments, fostering a deeper understanding of theoretical concepts and honing their analytical and problem-solving skills in the realm of physical sciences.

~ CHEMISTRY LAB ~

Within the school, the chemistry laboratory stands as a dedicated space for students to immerse themselves in practical applications of chemical principles. Well-equipped with instruments and safety measures, the lab fosters a hands-on approach to learning, allowing students to conduct experiments, analyze reactions, and deepen their understanding of chemistry concepts. This practical engagement cultivates scientific curiosity and critical thinking skills, enriching the overall learning experience in the field of chemistry.

~ BIOLOGY LAB ~

The school's biology laboratory is a dynamic space designed to nurture students' curiosity in the living world. Stocked with specimens, microscopes, and other essential tools, the lab provides a hands-on environment for students to explore biological concepts through experiments and dissections. This immersive experience not only enhances their understanding of life sciences but also fosters a deeper appreciation for the intricate workings of the natural world.

~ GROUND ~

The school proudly boasts a spacious playground, providing students with a vibrant outdoor setting for recreational activities and physical exercise. This open area serves as a hub for sports, games, and social interactions, contributing to the overall well-being and development of students in a lively and engaging atmosphere.

~ PARK ~

Nestled within the school premises is a serene park, offering students a tranquil retreat for relaxation and outdoor leisure. This green oasis provides a peaceful environment for reflection, socializing, and appreciating nature, enhancing the overall ambiance of the school campus.

~ SMART CLASSES ~

The school is equipped with modern smart classrooms, integrating technology to enhance the learning experience. These technologically advanced classrooms facilitate interactive and engaging lessons, utilizing multimedia resources to make education more dynamic and effective. Smart classes contribute to a progressive and forward-thinking educational environment, aligning with the evolving needs of 21st-century learners.
